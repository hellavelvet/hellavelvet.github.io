Description

Digital media quintet Hella Velvet is a collection of programmers, artists and musicians that met in Athens and Atlanta, GA. Their work includes games, projection media, and virtual reality experiences. Having teamed up on projects in the past, Hella Velvet formed in early 2017. Their first project Voiceball is an elaborate ploy to convince people to make weird mouth sounds.
